Welcome to the Santa Clara University Chess Club! Our club is dedicated to promoting the love of chess among students, faculty, and the broader community. Whether you're a seasoned grandmaster or a curious beginner, you'll find a place here to challenge your mind, hone your skills, and make lasting friendships. Join us for weekly games, tournaments, and events that will take your chess game to the next level. Let's make every move count! ♟️
